How to install
-----------------

You should be looking at the Andrea folder right now if your reading this; if your not unzip the archive. You should than see a folder called andrea. 

Open up your FTP program and login to your server. Navigate to your WordPress folder and upload the entire folder (andrea) to your wp-content/themes folder.

Login to WordPress administration normally and select Presentation from the menu.

You should see a freshly installed theme called Andrea 0.3 and a few basic details about the theme. 

Active this theme by clicking Select on the right.

View your site to see The Journalist in all his glory. 
	
Note: A page refresh may be required.
